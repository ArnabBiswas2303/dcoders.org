ABOUT US



Aim

The D'coderS Group aims to promote an active and dynamic coding culture in campus and encourage students to explore the fun side of software programming.



Objectives

• Encouraging an experimental method to learn how to code better
• Introducing concepts like FOSS, Big Data, Front-End Development and encouraging developments in the same via bimonthly Code & Design Meets
• Developing the culture of participating in competitive coding competitions across India such as ACM-ICPC.
• Working with Groups such as Mozilla Community and GDG India to introduce new local and global tech initiatives in Sikkim.
• Maintaining Online Code repositories that will act as academic supplements for all students interested in CSE.



Benefits

Promotion of greater role of “Tech” in the SMIT Campus. Encouraging Student Contribution to Open Source Projects hosted by Companies such as Mozilla, Canonical, Google etc. Introducing an excitement for writing and experimenting with Code, especially amongst freshmen students. Connecting SMIT to the World, though National and International Competitions & Events hosted by Mozilla, ACM, CodeChef, IITs amongst others.
